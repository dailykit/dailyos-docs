# Introduction

DailyOS Recipe app allows you to create and manage recipes with ease.

Recipe app host all your recipes in one place, where you can create, edit, search and scale all your recipes. Recipe app also allows read-only accounts for your staff in the kitchen. The Recipe app has two modules:

 1. Recipes
 2. Ingredients

![Recipe App](/images/recipe/recipe-home.png "Recipe App Home Screen")

## Some Salient Features
 - Add Recipe Information for multiple servings
 - Add Information related to cuisine, chef, utensils


## Why do you need Recipe Management?

Recipes are the soul of any food business and operations. 
The art of writing recipes have been practiced from ages and this time is no different.
