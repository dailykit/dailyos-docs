DailyOS is a powerful suite of enterprise grade apps that allow you and your team to manage your food business with utmost ease.

App Name | Description
---|---

Store | Manage your Online Store
Inventory | End to End Inventory Management
Order | Central Order and Production Management
Safety | Manage Staff health and Sanitation tests
Settings | Manage overall settings of your system
