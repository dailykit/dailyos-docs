# Product Types

## DailyKIT allows you to sell 4 types of product 

**[Simple Recipe product](/docs/store/simple-recipe-product)**: The simple recipe product contains only 1 recipe.  

**Customizable product**: This product can have mulitple recipe options with default on one. 

**Inventory product**: This product can contain your inventory items like grocery and beverages directly to be sold. 

**The combo product**: this product can sell multiple items like rice and daal together, pasta and wine together. You can set pricing as well as discount on serving level
